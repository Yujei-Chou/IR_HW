It is likely many will begin this New Year with some trepidation and skepticism as the past two years have been driven by crisis and chaos from the COVID-19 pandemic. The National Association of Chronic Diseases Directors and Gallup Inc., collaborated to create a dashboard looking at health behaviors related to coping with the realities of the pandemic and found that approximately 43% of Americans report worse exercise habits compared to before COVID-19, 31% report worse dietary habits, and 31% report that financial hardship has prevented them from seeking health care. Nurses answered the call for crucial changes in care delivery; policy shifts and pushed forward to develop innovative ways to meet patients where they were.
