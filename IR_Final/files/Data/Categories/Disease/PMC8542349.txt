The COVID-19 pandemic has drawn attention to food insecurity in developed countries. Social responsibility of manufacturers is recognized as part of the response, and strategies for mitigation of high-demand essential non-food items during a pandemic situation have been proposed. In summary, the best mitigation strategy due to the amount of processed food likely consumed is to improve its nutritional value.
