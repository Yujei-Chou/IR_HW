Here, we describe the educational, curricular, technical, and administrative aspects of our virtual telesimulation elective in an effort to provide other medical schools facing similar challenges with a blueprint for developing active distance learning opportunities for clerkship-level students. Before the first session, students viewed an orientation video describing the mechanics of the virtual telesimulation experience.
