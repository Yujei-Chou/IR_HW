Coronavirus disease (COVID-19), which was first recorded in China in December 2019, quickly spread to other countries and in a short period of time, the local outbreak escalated into a pandemic. To confirm our hypothesis of the possible protective role of genotype II carriers in SARS-CoV-2 infection affecting thus the course of COVID-19 disease, we analyzed the correlation between morbidity and mortality per one million population and the frequency of genotype II of the ACE1 gene.
