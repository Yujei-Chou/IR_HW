Severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus-2 (SARS-CoV-2) has emerged into a worldwide pandemic of epic proportion. Kidney biopsies performed in patients with COVID-19 have revealed diverse glomerular and tubular disorders. Interferon mRNA is not detected in kidney biopsies with collapsing glomerulopathy, consistent with renal pathology resulting from a systemic innate immune response to COVID-19.
