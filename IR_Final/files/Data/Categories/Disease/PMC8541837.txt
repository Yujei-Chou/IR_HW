The COVID-19 pandemic has had a significant impact on surgical specialties. Prioritizing IPC in the planning of surgical services during the COVID-19 pandemic has been crucial to ensure patient and staff safety. A letter from NHS England dated 24 June 2020 advised retesting patients 5-7 days after admission, with the aim of identifying those patients who are incubating an infection acquired in the community but test negative on admission.
