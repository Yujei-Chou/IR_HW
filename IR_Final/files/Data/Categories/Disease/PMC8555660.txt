Introduction: The pandemics crisis had consequences in psychological adjustment of persons all over the world. The survey comprises questions related to mental health and well-being in several domains of daily life. Example of items: "In comparison to before the Coronavirus outbreak, I feel like I'm doing something for society"; In comparison to before the Coronavirus outbreak, I am more concerned about my partnership/family" (1 = "not at all," 2 = "somewhat," 3, = "moderately," 4 = "very much").General well-being was measured using ten items taken from the Well-being Manifestations Measure Scale (adaptation by, of the original EMBEPP by), ranging from none of the time to all of the time, rated on a 5-point Likert-type intensity scale.
