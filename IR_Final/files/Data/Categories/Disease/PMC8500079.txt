Abstract
Novel coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) outbreak started from Wuhan, China in December 2019 with many cases of pneumonia of unknown causes. Thus, time is an important aspect, where timely administration of medical care ensures treatment efficacy. A secondary aspect of this study was to observe the effect of time-dependent use of corticosteroids on mortality of critical/severe COVID-19 patients.
