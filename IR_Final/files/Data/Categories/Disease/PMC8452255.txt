The visiting professor is an important tradition in plastic and reconstructive surgery. Following relaxing of COVID-19 social distancing, six of seven visiting professors (85.7 percent) would like to continue virtual visiting professorships (Tables 1 and 2).When surveyed on their experiences, six of 17 residents (35.3 percent) preferred in-person visiting professors and five of 17 (29.4 percent) preferred virtual visiting professors. The visiting professor sacrifices clinical and research opportunities including missed relative value unit earning potential.
