Of the 50 respondents who had in-person encounter in an inpatient setting, 33% or 66% of respondents reported at least 1 encounter (7 had 1 encounter, 13 had 2 to 5 encounters, and 13 had >5 encounters) with COVID-19 patients within the last 2 weeks.
