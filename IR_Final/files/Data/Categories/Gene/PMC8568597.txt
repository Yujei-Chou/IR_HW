Since coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) has continued to spread globally, many countries have started vaccinations at the end of December 2020. These two measures were proposed to estimate countries' capability to maximize vaccination distribution to prevent new pandemic threats. There is one year lag between independent variables (i.e., GDP per capita and HDI), and the outcome was mainly estimated in 2021.
