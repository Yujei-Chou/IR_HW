Background
Since the outbreak of the coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) in 2020, the United States suffered over 32 million infections, with an estimated death toll from COVID-19 of well over half a million as of April 2021. A meta-analysis, covering 136 studies of populations affected by COVID-19 found that at least 15-16% of the general population experienced symptoms of anxiety or depression.
