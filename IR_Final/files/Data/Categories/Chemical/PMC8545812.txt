The vulnerability of employees of different occupations from the Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) pandemic in Iran was assessed using an innovative index. The initial list of principles and components of employees' susceptibility and resilience in the COVID-19 pandemic was prepared by reviewing the literature and brain storming. The susceptibility, resilience, and vulnerability index values were classified into the five categories as very high (80-100), high (65-79), medium (50-64), low (30-49), and very low (0-29).The susceptibility, resilience, and vulnerability of employees of different occupations in the COVID-19 pandemic at the national level were assessed using the above-mentioned equations.
