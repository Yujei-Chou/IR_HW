To the editor:
To the editor:In the article by Sanchez Gomez et al. Studies conducted in different countries focused on how eating habits, physical activity and weight gain were affected by the COVID-19 pandemic. With regard to physical activity, prior to the pandemic 32.1% did no physical exercise, a percentage that increased to 36.5% during quarantine periods; 40.3% also noticed an increase in body weight.
