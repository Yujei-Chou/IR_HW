The authors have no conflict of interest to declare. Herein, we describe a case of mainly guttate psoriasis after a COVID-19 vaccination. A 79-year-old female patient was referred to our department due to a disseminated itching psoriasiform rash, which had started 10 days after receiving the first injection with the COVID-19 vaccination Comirnaty  (BioNTech, Freiburgstrasse, Bern, Switzerland).
