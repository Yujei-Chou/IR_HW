Newly emerging infectious diseases, such as the coronavirus (COVID-19), create new challenges for public healthcare systems. The health belief model (HBM) assumes that health behaviour depends on the perceived threat, the perceived benefits of health behaviour, and the perceived cost of health behaviour, comprising economic, social, and psychological costs, such as anxiety, shame, or discomfort (Rosenstock,; Becker et al.,).
