To the EditorIn recent publications reacting to Neal's collateral damage of COVID-19, thoughts on the importance of spirituality were highlighted as one of the salvific factors of men and women of faith. Due to strict and prolonged quarantine orders to stay home, unavailability of online communication channels and information on where and how to report, the victims are placed at greater risk. The less fortunate sector of the society is another collateral damage.
