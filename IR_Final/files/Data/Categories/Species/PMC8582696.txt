Aim: An online survey was proposed to the health workers of a public hospital of an Italian northern city. Regarding their COVID-19 psychological and professional experience, the results of the survey's quantitative part showed that many HCWs reported feelings of uncertainty (40.16%), fear that the people they care about may get sick (65.47%) and worries of being infected (27.03%) as the main pandemic difficulties.
