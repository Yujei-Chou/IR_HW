Data on the coincidence of tuberculosis (TB) and COVID-19 are limited, and previous observations are based on the results of just a few studies, which has led to polarized views on the course of infection with SARS-CoV-2 in patients with active TB. The patient was treated for tuberculosis in 1981.A test for SARS-CoV-2 was performed using a throat and nasal swab taken on 27 May 2020. Polarized views have been reported regarding the course of SARS-CoV-2 infection in patients with active TB.
