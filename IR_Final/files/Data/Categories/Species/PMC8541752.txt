The aim of this research is to analyse the way young people perceive the food waste process, as well as the determinants and the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on the responsible behaviour of young people towards food waste. Our research analyses the impact of COVID-19 pandemic on the young people's responsible behaviour to food waste.
