Vaccination behavior is an informative metric for assessing flu seasons and is especially important to understand for the 2020-2021 flu season, which coincided with the COVID-19 pandemic. This study aims to estimate flu vaccine uptake and analyze vaccine hesitancy for the 2020-2021 flu season, as well as to assess the impact of the concurrent COVID-19 pandemic on such behaviors.
