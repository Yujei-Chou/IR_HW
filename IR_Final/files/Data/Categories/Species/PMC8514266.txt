The COVID-19 outbreak is having a profound impact on individuals' psychological and physical wellbeing. Against this background, the goal of this study was to establish the role that emotion regulation and individual factors play in shaping health-related behaviours at the time of COVID-19. A psychological vulnerability score was also created based on the answers provided to the questions "Have you ever suffered from mental health difficulties?"
